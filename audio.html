STUDIOS

A lot goes into making a great record.   An unbeatable selection of audio tools and a comfortable, creative vibe make these rooms a special place to make music in New York City.   Live bands, vocal tracking, writing/production sessions and more - We have everything you need to create.

ENGINEER

I love working with sound. I take pride in everything from selecting the perfect mics to perfecting the final mix. Yet, when it comes to making a record, I believe the engineer's job is to stand clear and let the artist do their thing. It's all about the performance.

I studied music at Columbia University and audio at the Institute of Audio Research. Since learning under veteran engineers and producers at the legendary Skyline Studios, I've spent ten years working with artists big and small in genres ranging from classical to hip-hop.
